EP RELEASES

Martial Taktics - Fight EP

Welcome to Bass Militia Records first official EP!

Leading the attack is Martial Taktics with his original track 'Fight'. Martial Taktics has been making a lot of noise in the scene in the past year and his track just proves what all the fuss is about!

On the remix duties we have some incredible work from Mutant Bass creator and legend, Kanji Kinetic. You know if he's on a track it means business and F**K how he has delivered!? Who's Peal you ask? Peal remixed this original track and is an up and coming talent on the scene. Some insane production skills from the man. Peal is definitely one to watch in the future. Maybe you will be seeing more of him through us, but we wouldn't be able to confirm that ;) Amen Shamen has earned his respect in the rave and club community by producing on point mash ups of Jungle and Hardtek. On the dancefloor he is a force to be reckoned with and we expect this track to blasted through sound systems all summer, causing havok in da dance!
